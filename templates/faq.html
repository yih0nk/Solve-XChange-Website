FAQ

Do you have a question about SolveXchange that you want
answered? Check out some Frequently Asked Questions that
students have commonly asked below.

Competition Specifics

What exactly do I have to create?

You must create a mandatory formal business proposal. Additional visual aids or creative components are recommended to be created to supplement the compulsory written document. This can include slideshows, presentations or creative videos. Itâ€™s up to you!

Can I compete in groups?

Yes! You are able to join the competition with a group of up to 4 members, with the option to work individually as well.

When do I have to submit my proposal?

The proposal deadline has been set to May 21, 2024. The final winner and runner-ups will be announced on May 31, 2024.

How often does SolveXchange run?

During the school year, we conduct two rounds of competitions, one from September to January, and the other from February to June. Students will have the opportunity to compete in both throughout the year. Lessons take place weekly throughout each term.

How do I know if I am eligible to compete?

Generally, students must be attending a secondary school (from grade 9-12) within Halton in order to compete. For more info, find the Eligibility guidelines on the Get Involved tab.

Funding

Will we get funding?

The winner of the competition will have $500 to work with in order to bring their ideas to life. This money is provided by the Halton District School Board and additional sponsors. Students are encouraged to plan a detailed budget and aim to use all of the funding available.

What if my idea needs more than the budget?

No problem! While we do want you to consider the viability aspect of your plan, we also want all participants to think big! If you find that your idea is ambitious and the costs exceed the budget, don't let this deter you! While we may not be able to fully fund your project, we are able to explore alternative avenues to help bring your idea to life. This includes arranging meetings with industry professionals, providing social media coverage, and assisting you in securing personal sponsors!

Benefits and Recognition

Why should I join, and how will this benefit me?

Submit your proposal to our community-wide competition for the opportunity to secure funding and turn your idea into reality. The winner will not only receive financial support but also be recognized by the HDSB, including a signed certificate from the Superintendent. Furthermore, there's a chance to be featured in Halton media, such as newspapers, and on our website.

By participating, you'll collaborate with like-minded individuals, forge new connections, and experience the meaningful impact of initiating change in your community.

Can I use this as something to add to my post-secondary applications?

Of course! Certainly! SolveXchange offers a valuable opportunity to learn and showcase your entrepreneurial skills, initiative, community action, collaboration and creativity. Participating in our competitions can provide a rich experience to enhance your applications. Feel free to connect with us on LinkedIn and add us to your profile!
