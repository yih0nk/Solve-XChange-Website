SolveXChange

Turning Vision into Reality, One Proposal at a Time

Innovate

Students spark novel ideas and approaches by creating
inventive solutions and utilizing problem-solving and creative
thinking skills.

Impact

Students make a tangible impact by implementing sustainable
solutions that address real-world challenges in their communities.

Inspire

Students inspire others by demonstrating dedication in serving
their community, and motivating peers to contribute to change.

“I’ve always heard students casually throw around valuable ideas to improve our community. Unfortunately, many of these ideas are often disregarded because students lack a platform to bring them to life. This became the main inspiration behind SolveXchange, to create an accessible platform with a focus on empowering students to become the driving force behind transformative change.”

- Emily Liu (SolveXchange Founder & President)
